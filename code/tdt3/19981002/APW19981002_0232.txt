Thai police have arrested a woman on charges of conducting an abortion 
on a 16-year-old girl, the newspaper The Nation reported Friday. Abortions 
are illegal in Thailand. Police found a 5-month-old fetus and a medical 
kit while raiding a clinic in the beach resort of Pattaya, 110 kilometers 
(68 miles) south of Bangkok, the newspaper said. A woman identified 
as Sumalee Tipeng, 52, was arrested and confessed to offering abortions 
at a price of 1,000 baht (dlrs 25.65) for each month the pregnancy 
had lasted, the newspaper said.
