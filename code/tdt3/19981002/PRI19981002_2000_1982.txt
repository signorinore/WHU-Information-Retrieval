Two months after the devastating bomb attack on the U.S. embassy in 
Kenya, the fund set up to help relatives of those who died has paid 
out its first benefits. About 30 close relatives received $1,700 at 
a ceremony in the main park in the capital, Nairobi. The fund now 
totals nearly $4 million and was established with donations from the 
public.
