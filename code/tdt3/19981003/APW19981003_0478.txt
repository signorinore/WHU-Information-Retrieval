Gustavo Kuerten of Brazil defeated Thomas Muster of Austria on Saturday 
to advance to the final of the 475,000 dollar Mallorca Open tennis 
tournament. Eighth-seeded Kuerten beat fifth seed Muster 7-5, 7-5 
in the first of the Saturday's semifinal singles matches. Carlos Moya 
_ the world No. 5, clear favorite and local hero from this Mediterranean 
Spanish island _ was due to meet fellow Spaniard Fernando Vicente 
in the other semifinal later Saturday.
