Swiss-Swedish engineering group Asea Brown Boveri said Monday it has 
won a contract worth more than dlrs 350 million to build a power plant 
in the United States. The order for a 1,000 megawatt power plant near 
Dallas, Texas was placed by American National Power Inc., a subsidiary 
of Britain's National Power PLC, ABB said in a statement. In addition 
to the engineering and construction work, as the engineering and construction 
work, ABB is to supply four gas turbines and associated steam turbines, 
electrical generators and heat recovery steam generators to the project. 
The plant will be one of the largest merchant power plants in the 
United States, delivering power according to short-term market demands 
rather than longer-term contracts.
