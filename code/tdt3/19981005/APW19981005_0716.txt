The Belarusian capital recorded its earliest snowfall of the year 
in 16 years on Monday. Snow has not fallen in Minsk on or before Oct. 
5 since 1972, said Tatiana Yatzkevich, head of the city's weather 
service. She could not say how much snow had fallen.
