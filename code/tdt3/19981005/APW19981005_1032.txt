Secretary-General Kofi Annan will visit Japan later this month to 
attend an African development conference, a spokesman said Monday. 
The second annual Tokyo international conference on African development 
is being organized by the Japanese government, the United Nations 
and a group called the Global Coalition for Africa. The U.N. chief 
will be in Japan from Oct. 20-22, spokesman Fred Eckhard said.
