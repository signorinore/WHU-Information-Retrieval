The city of Angels paid its respects for the man who sat in city hall 
for 25 years. Former mayor Tom Bradley's funeral was held this morning. 
The 1500 mourners were led by vice President Al Gore who praised Bradley 
as a healer. Also paying their respect, Pete Wilson and Reverend Jesse 
Jackson among so many others. Bradley died of an heart attack last 
Tuesday. He was 80 years old and had been in failing health.
