Hungary's Foreign Minister Janos Martonyi will visit Ukraine this 
week, Ukraine's Foreign Ministry said Tuesday. It said Martonyi's 
Oct. 11-12 visit would be devoted to preparations for Ukrainian President 
Leonid Kuchma's official visit to Hungary, scheduled for Oct. 27-28. 
In Hungary, Kuchma plans to discuss bilateral issues and international 
political and economic developments.
