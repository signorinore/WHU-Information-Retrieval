The big apple played host to a big Strawberry treat today. Kellogg's 
unveiled the world's largest pop Tart at Madison square garden to 
commemorate the pastry's 35th anniversary. More than 545 pounds of 
flour, 495 pounds of filling, 800 pounds of icing and 150 pounds of 
sprinkles went into making the giant Tart. It measured 25' x 35' and 
was available for sampling by morning commuters.
