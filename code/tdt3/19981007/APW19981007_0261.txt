Three people died and four were injured when a boiler exploded at 
a beer-producing factory in Simferopol, the capital of Ukraine's Crimean 
Peninsula, emergency officials said Wednesday. The blast, which also 
damaged a section of the Krympivo plant, occurred late Tuesday, said 
the Emergency Situations Ministry. The cause of the accident was not 
immediately clear.
