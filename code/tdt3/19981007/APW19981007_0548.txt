The Israeli and Palestinian leaders made ``substantial progress'' 
toward a West Bank pullback Wednesday and will continue their talks 
at an open-ended Washington summit starting Oct. 15, U.S. Secretary 
of State Madeleine Albright announced. Albright spoke after Israeli 
Prime Minister Benjamin Netanyahu and Palestinian leader Yasser Arafat 
met for nearly four hours in their most extensive talks in a year. 
Albright said that a ``new spirit was very evident'' in the meetings.
