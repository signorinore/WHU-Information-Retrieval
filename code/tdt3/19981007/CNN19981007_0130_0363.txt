Chrysler is recalling 685,000 cars that can roll away under certain 
conditions when left in park. The recall covers the Chrysler Cirrus 
and Dodge stratus models from 1995 through '98 and the Plymouth breeze 
and Chrysler sebring made from 1996 through '98. Chrysler will notify 
affected drivers in December. Chrysler is also recalling 157,000 factory 
installed child seats. The affected models are the Chrysler town and 
country, Dodge caravan and grand caravan and the Plymouth Voyager 
and grand Voyager minivans for years 1996 and '97.
