At least seven French tourists died Thursday when a boat carrying 
141 sightseers went down in a lake in northeastern Spain, emergency 
services said. The reasons why the boat sank at 10.45 a.m. (0845GMT) 
in the Banyoles lake near the city of Girona where not yet known, 
a spokeswoman for the local emergency services said. The passengers 
were from La Rochelle in France, the spokeswoman said.
