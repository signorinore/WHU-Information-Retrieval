Secretive North Korea poses a threat to the proliferation of missiles, 
a watchdog organization said Friday. The 32-member Missile Technology 
Control Regime released its warning after a two-day conference. It 
said the organization was particularly concerned about the potential 
of a North Korean rocket test launched Aug. 31, for delivering weapons 
of mass destruction over a long distance. MTCR was established in 
1987, and has, among others, government representatives of the United 
States, Canada, Japan, France, Germany, Russia, as members Its aim 
is to control exports of missiles capable of carrying nuclear, biological 
and chemical weapons, as well as the relevant technology.
